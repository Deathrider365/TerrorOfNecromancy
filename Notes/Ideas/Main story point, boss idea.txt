there was a decent government on Vita and the castle that the necromancer takes over is the remnant of that.
Inspired by Sir Alonne from DS2 there was a king trying to control his lands from all of the turmoil, then a
mysterious warrior comes and helps him outt. After the king and his warrior take control and govern peace, the
king becomes drunk under the power and starts to be more brutal to his people and land, which results in the 
warrior challenging him, wanting to take him down out of his guilt of helping such a selfish brutal man.
The warrior succeeds and the old king becomes the leader of the Undead Legion, given he dabbled in some
dark magic and upon his death becomes undead along with his legion. The aim to take the warrior down to
regain control but fail, and are banished by the warrior.
